More than just UX, I see myself as a Human-centered designer. It is my duty and aspiration to define the ways humans relate to and interact with the world, how we can navigate space effectively, and how we are able to get our needs and demands met in this increasingly product-focused world.

About Me

Experience Matters.

Over the course of 6 years, I have successfully transformed myself from my previous expertise as a digital marketing consultant into becoming an interaction designer a strong emphasis in UX Research, UI Design, human factors comprehension, and customer experience. I have delivered various digital projects from clients in different industries in Germany, China, Japan, Indonesia, Spain, and the USA, from web and mobile apps interface design, to design system, corporate branding, and large-scale user experience researches.

Skills.

I firmly believe in the application of empathic approaches and psychological principles to the design and engineering of products, processes, and systems in order to increase its value, productivity, safety, accessibility, and usability.

UX Design

The design of the entire process of acquiring and integrating the product, including aspects of usability and function.

Front-end

Determining the structure and developing thedesign of web pages, striking a balance between usability and aesthetic design.

UI Design

Designing graphical user interfaces in software or computerized devices with a focus on looks or style.

UX Research

Using variety of investigative methods used to add context and insight to the design process.

Product Management

Dealing with new product development, business justification, planning, and marketing of a product at all stages.

Graphic Design

Process of visual identity communication of the product through unique typography, iconography and illustration.
