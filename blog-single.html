UX Panelist: Indonesia's UX Meetup with Dunia Dalam Desain

I was invited to speak at @duniadalamdesain meet up tomorrow alongside two female UX Designers from Indonesia's hottest startups @gojekindonesia and @tokopedia.

Dunia Dalam Desain, translated to 'World Within Design' is the biggest UX Community in Indonesia.

It is well known for its podcast 'DDD Podcast' that tackles various design problems and highlights Indonesian Designers all over the world. I was honored to be invited to talk for the podcast last August.

Finding UX Community back home has made my homecoming to Jakarta memorable in 2019. I am very thankful for the chance and the experience that DDD has given to me.

Thanks again DDD and everyone who attended the meetup. Hope to see everyone again this year!
