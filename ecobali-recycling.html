Ecobali Recycling App

UX Design Case Study

Posted by Sasha Astiadi on March 19, 2018

Time: February - May 2018
Platform: Mobile App
UX Tools used: Axure RP, Figma

During my admission process to CODE University, I was challenged to develop a digital approach to solve an organization’s problem and support their missions digitally. As UX designer, I chose to design EcoBali’s waste management app from scratch. EcoBali is an environmental NGO that focuses on initiating sustainable waste-management program by providing recycling services in Bali, Indonesia.

Range of Work

  • Competitive Analysis
  • Affinity Mapping
  • User Personas Writing
  • User Task Flows
  • Moodboard
  • Paper Prototypes
  • Lo-fi Axure RP Prototypes
  • Hi-fi Figma Prototypes
  • User Testing
